BI Project - Group 3

Introduction

Our warehouse is designed with the needs of artists and music labels/producers in mind. They should be able to perform analytics on the charts with various time and regional granularities. They will need to analyse trends for example the rising popularity of a genre in a specific country or region. The warehouse will also be used to perform more open explorations as we aim to identify the importance of different features in song popularity. This warehouse will therefore need to be focused on large aggregation over time/genre/region/artists and will not contain user specific informations. Those needs will guide our DW design process, we will use the needs driven approach.
